Certification Prep Pro is a native study app for the AWS Cloud Practitioner and Solutions Architect Associate exams. It keeps 1,300+ practice questions, timed exams, quick quizzes, and progress tracking in one focused place on iPhone and Mac.

## The problem

AWS exam preparation usually means switching between browser tabs, question banks, notes, and timers. I wanted a study loop that made it easy to answer a useful set of questions, review weak areas, and return later without rebuilding that context.

## What it does

- **Two exam tracks:** AWS Cloud Practitioner and Solutions Architect Associate (SAA-C03)
- **1,300+ questions:** more than 600 Cloud Practitioner questions and 700+ SAA-C03 questions
- **Two session lengths:** 65-question practice exams or 20-question quick quizzes
- **Timed practice:** exam-style timers and a clear 75% pass indicator
- **Useful review:** performance broken down by domain and topic
- **Native on both platforms:** study on iPhone and continue on Mac

## The build

The app uses Swift with native SwiftUI and AppKit interfaces for iOS 17.5+ and macOS 14.5+. Sharing the study model across platforms keeps progress and exam behavior consistent, while platform-specific UI preserves the conventions people expect on each device.

## Result

Certification Prep Pro is available on the App Store as a distraction-free alternative to browser-based question banks. The product stays deliberately narrow: open it, practice, see where you are weak, and get back to studying.
